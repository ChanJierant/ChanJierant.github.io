  1. 163:
    (1)、首先备份/etc/yum.repos.d/CentOS-Base.repo
    [root@XXOO yum.repos.d]# mv /etc/yum.repos.d/CentOS-Base.repo /etc/yum.repos.d/CentOS-Base.repo.backup
    (2)、下载对应版本repo文件, 放入/etc/yum.repos.d/(操作前请做好相应备份)
    [root@XXOO yum.repos.d]# wget -O /etc/yum.repos.d/CentOS-Base.repo http://mirrors.163.com/.help/CentOS7-Base-163.repo
